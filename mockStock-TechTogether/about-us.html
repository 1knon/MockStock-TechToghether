About Us

Click here to find our app!

Mock Stock is an organisation that thrives to teach youth how to invest into the stock market through interactive games and material.

We are both a web-based and app-based product.

Our app:

  • Contains a point system where users can invest their points into stocks which can output either a positive or negative result
  • Stimulates the real world market through informing players about recent news
  • Gives users a set time limit in order to make the best decisions for themeselves

Our website aims to:

  1. Give youth insights into careers they can go into that work with the stock market
  2. Keep everyone up to date on events that happen in the stock market
  3. Help youth understand how the stock market works through resources

We hope this was helpful and make sure to check out the rest of our website to invest into your future!
